Company-Managed Placement (Indirect)

The Sethakga Group manages the fairy on your behalf:

  • 50% deposit required before services commence
  • Remaining 50% payable at month-end for the first month
  • Thereafter, full payment due at month-end upon invoice
  • Fairy supervision and coordination handled by The Sethakga Group
  • Standby Fairy Available: A standby fairy may be provided when your regular fairy is unavailable
Client-Managed Placement (Direct)

The client manages the fairy directly while The Sethakga Group handles recruitment and placement:

  • Fairy Salary Requirement: Minimum monthly pay for the fairy must be R5,000.00 or more
  • Placement Fee: Sethakga placement fee is 50% of the fairy's agreed monthly salary
  • Recruitment Deposit: A 50% deposit of the placement fee is required to begin the recruitment process
  • Balance Payment: The remaining placement fee is payable once the fairy has commenced duties
  • SLA: 3-5 working days to present suitable candidates
  • Payment Structure: Client pays the fairy directly
  • Replacement Guarantee: One fairy replacement allowed within 2 months if not satisfied
