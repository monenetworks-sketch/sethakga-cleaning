About the Academy

Built to Unlock Potential

The Sethakga Academy was founded on a simple belief — every young person deserves access to real skills that open real doors. We don't just teach; we equip, mentor, and launch.

Youth Empowerment

Focused on young people aged 18–35 who are unemployed or under-employed. We provide structured training as a pathway into dignified, sustainable work.

Hands-On Learning

No classroom theory without practice. Every course includes real-world, hands-on components so students leave with confidence and a working portfolio.

Employment Pathways

Top graduates are supported in finding employment or starting their own micro-enterprises — either through Sethakga's network or partner businesses.

Gauteng-Based

Based in Midrand with access across Gauteng. Training is conducted at our facilities and in the field — students learn in real environments alongside real clients.

Skills Change Lives

A skill is the most portable asset a person can own. At Sethakga Academy, we build competence, confidence, and character — preparing graduates who are not just employable, but entrepreneurially minded.

Our Courses

What You Can Learn

Practical courses designed around real industry needs — taught by experienced Sethakga professionals

Professional Cleaning

Deep cleaning techniques, chemical safety, equipment handling, carpet & upholstery cleaning, pest control awareness, and post-construction cleanup.

4–6 Weeks

Hair Braiding & Styling

Box braids, cornrows, twists, weave & wig installation, natural hair care, and client consultation skills. Taught by certified Sethakga stylists.

6–8 Weeks

Maid & Domestic Services

Home management, household etiquette, laundry & ironing, childcare basics, employer communication, and professional conduct in domestic settings.

3–4 Weeks

Basic Electronics & Repairs

Introduction to electronics, basic wiring, fault-finding, appliance repair fundamentals, and safety practices — opening doors to the technical sector.

8–10 Weeks

Commercial Cleaning & Facilities

Office and industrial cleaning standards, contract management basics, client relations, health & safety compliance, and team coordination.

4–6 Weeks

Entrepreneurship & Business Basics

Starting a service business, pricing, invoicing, client relationships, basic bookkeeping, social media marketing, and building a brand from the ground up.

4 Weeks

Pest Control Basics

Residential and commercial pest identification, safe chemical usage, fumigation processes, compliance essentials, and customer communication.

2–3 Weeks

Post-Construction Cleanup

Specialised techniques for clearing construction sites — dust, rubble, paint residue — using industrial equipment and following handover protocols.

3 Weeks
