# The Sethakga Group - Professional Brand Color System

## 🎨 Brand Color Architecture

### **The Sethakga Group** (Parent Brand)
**Colors:** Gold, Black, White
- **Primary Gold:** `#D4AF37` (Royal gold for premium feel)
- **Gold Dark:** `#B8941E` (Hover states)
- **Gold Light:** `#F1D678` (Highlights)
- **Gold Pale:** `#FFF9E6` (Backgrounds)

**Usage:**
- Main navigation highlights
- Hero section accents
- Awards and achievements
- Premium CTAs and buttons
- Trust bar gradient background

### **Sethakga Cleaning**
**Colors:** Pink, Black, White
- **Primary Pink:** `#C41E6B` (Vibrant, energetic)
- **Pink Light:** `#E8417E` (Hover effects)
- **Pink Pale:** `#FDF0F5` (Soft backgrounds)

**Usage:**
- Cleaning service cards
- Cleaning section headers
- Cleaning-specific badges
- Cleaning CTAs (`.btn-cleaning`)

### **Sethakga Hair**
**Colors:** Purple, Black, White
- **Primary Purple:** `#7B3FF2` (Luxury, creativity)
- **Purple Dark:** `#6200EA` (Deep accents)
- **Purple Light:** `#9D65F6` (Highlights)
- **Purple Pale:** `#F3E5F5` (Backgrounds)

**Usage:**
- Hair service cards
- Hair section headers
- Hair-specific badges
- Hair CTAs (`.btn-hair`)

## 🎯 CSS Classes Quick Reference

### Buttons
- `.btn-primary` - Gold button (Sethakga Group)
- `.btn-cleaning` - Pink button (Cleaning services)
- `.btn-hair` - Purple button (Hair services)
- `.btn-outline` - Transparent with white border
- `.btn-outline-pink` - Pink outline
- `.btn-dark` - Dark button

### Brand Badges
- `.brand-badge--group` - Gold badge
- `.brand-badge--cleaning` - Pink badge
- `.brand-badge--hair` - Purple badge

### Section Tags
- `.section-tag` - Default (gold)
- `.section-tag--cleaning` - Pink tag
- `.section-tag--hair` - Purple tag

### Special Effects
- `.gradient-text` - Multi-brand gradient text
- `.achievement-badge` - Gold achievement badge
- `.hover-glow--gold` - Gold glow on hover
- `.hover-glow--pink` - Pink glow on hover
- `.hover-glow--purple` - Purple glow on hover

## 💡 Professional Design Principles Applied

1. **Brand Hierarchy:** Parent brand (gold) sits above divisions (pink, purple)
2. **Visual Distinction:** Each division has clear visual identity
3. **Cohesion:** All brands share black/white, creating unity
4. **Premium Feel:** Gold elevates the overall brand perception
5. **Accessibility:** High contrast ratios maintained
6. **Consistency:** Systematic application across all pages
7. **Sophistication:** Gradient transitions and smooth animations
