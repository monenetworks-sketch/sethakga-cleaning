How the Referral Programme Works

Three easy steps to start earning rewards for yourself and giving a gift to someone you care about.

01

Share Your Link

Tell a friend, family member, neighbour, or colleague about The Sethakga Group. Share our number, website, or social media pages.

02

They Book & Clean

Your referral books and completes their first cleaning or hair braiding service with us. They must mention your name when booking.

03

You Both Get Rewarded

You receive your reward on your next booking. Your referral also gets a welcome discount on their first service. Win-win!
