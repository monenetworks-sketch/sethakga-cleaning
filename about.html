Our Story

Born from a Passion for
Pristine Spaces

Founded in May 2019 by Kelotilwe Roberta Legwabe, The Sethakga Group was built on a genuine love for creating clean, organized, and peaceful spaces. What started as a personal passion has grown into a trusted, award-winning cleaning service across Gauteng — crowned Service Business of the Year at the News24 Lula SME Awards 2025.

"To help households and companies have an immaculate space where they can be productive and feel at ease."

Our Journey

The Sethakga Timeline

From humble beginnings to an award-winning business serving all of Gauteng.

1

May 2019 — Founded

Kelotilwe Roberta Legwabe launches The Sethakga Group from Silverstream Estate, Midrand, with a vision to create immaculate spaces.

2

2019 — First Clients

Began serving residential clients in Midrand and Vorna Valley with deep cleaning and maid services.

3

2020 — Expanding Services

Added carpet & upholstery steaming, pest control, and commercial cleaning. Expanded across Gauteng.

4

2021 — Certificate of Excellence

Awarded the Certificate of Excellence by Bark.com — recognising outstanding client satisfaction.

5

2022–2024 — Continued Growth

Growing team, wider service area, and an ever-expanding client base across Gauteng.

6

2025 — Service Business of the Year

Awarded Service Business of the Year at the News24 Lula SME Awards 2025.
