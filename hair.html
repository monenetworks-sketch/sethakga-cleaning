What We Offer

Our Braiding Services

From classic box braids to intricate cornrow designs — professional hairstyling services delivered to your doorstep.

Braiding & Protective Styles

Box Braids

Classic protective style with individual square-sectioned braids. Available in small, medium, or jumbo sizes with various lengths.

  • Small, Medium, or Jumbo thickness
  • Short, Medium, or Long length
  • Synthetic or human hair extensions
  • Lasts 6-8 weeks
→ Most Popular Style

Cornrows

Traditional African braids plaited close to the scalp in straight lines or intricate patterns. Perfect for all ages and occasions.

  • Straight-back cornrows
  • Feed-in method (natural look)
  • Zig-zag or curved patterns
  • Lasts 2-4 weeks
→ Adults & Children

Twists (Senegalese, Havana, Marley)

Elegant two-strand twists using premium quality hair extensions. Choose from Senegalese, Havana, or Marley twist styles.

  • Senegalese twists (sleek finish)
  • Havana twists (chunky, voluminous)
  • Marley twists (natural texture)
  • Lasts 4-8 weeks
→ Multiple Twist Types

Passion Twists & Spring Twists

Trendy bohemian-style twists with natural texture and bounce. Lightweight and easy to maintain.

  • Water wave texture
  • Curly spring finish
  • Lightweight & comfortable
  • Lasts 6-8 weeks
→ Trendy Bohemian Look

Weaves & Installations

Weave Installation (Sew-In)

Professional weave installation with secure cornrow foundation. Bring your own bundles or we can supply premium quality hair.

  • Full or partial sew-in
  • Leave-out or closure options
  • All hair textures
→ Bring Your Own or We Supply

Wig Installation & Customization

Expert wig installation with plucking, bleaching knots, and edge customization for a natural finish. Lace frontal or full lace wigs.

  • Hairline customization
  • Knot bleaching & plucking
  • Secure installation
→ Natural Look Guaranteed

Closure / Frontal Installation

Professional closure or frontal installation for seamless, natural-looking weaves with versatile styling options.

  • 4x4, 5x5, or 6x6 closures
  • 13x4 or 13x6 frontals
  • Bleached knots & plucking
→ Versatile Parting

Weave Maintenance & Wash

Keep your weave fresh and clean with professional maintenance services — deep wash, condition, blow-dry, and style.

→ Extend Your Hairstyle's Life
