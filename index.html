What We Do

Our Cleaning Services

From residential deep cleans to industrial solutions — we tailor every job to your specific needs, leaving every space spotless.

Residential Deep Cleaning

Full-house cleaning including walls, stoves, ovens, windows, and cupboards. Every corner, every surface — thoroughly cleaned.

→ Homes & Apartments

Maid Services

Flexible housekeeping options — weekly, monthly, or full-time. Customised schedules designed around your lifestyle.

→ Weekly / Monthly / Full-time

Carpet & Upholstery Steaming

Professional steam deep-cleaning for carpets, mattresses, couches, and car seats. Restore freshness and hygiene.

→ Carpets · Couches · Car Seats

Commercial & Office Cleaning

Tailored cleaning solutions for businesses and offices. Create a productive, healthy, and impressive workspace.

→ Offices · Business Premises

Pre & Post-Construction Cleaning

Move-in ready cleaning after construction or renovation. Remove dust, debris, and residue — ready for occupation.

→ New Builds · Renovations

Pest Control & Fumigation

Effective pest control and fumigation services to keep your home or business safe, clean, and pest-free.

→ Residential & Commercial

Window Cleaning

Streak-free, crystal-clear windows inside and out. Let the light in and the grime out — for homes and offices.

→ Interior & Exterior

Industrial Cleaning

Heavy-duty solutions including sludge removal and workshop cleaning. Built for demanding industrial environments.

→ Workshops · Industrial Sites
